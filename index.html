Etiquette

Feel free to mix styles, innovate, or even create a category of your own.

  • Formal: Elegant attire that speaks of timeless sophistication.
  • Hippie: Embrace the freedom and romanticism of the '60s and '70s.
  • Casual: Comfortable yet tasteful, perfect for a relaxed gathering.
  • Artista: Let your attire be your canvas – bold, colorful, and expressive.
  • Fallero: Inspired by Valencia's traditional costumes, embody the festivity and cultural richness.
  • Carnavalero: Capture the exuberance of carnival with vibrant colors and festive accessories.

Siente la libertad de innovar o incluso crear una categoría propia.

  • Hippie: Abraza la libertad y el romanticismo de los años '60 y '70.
  • Casual: Cómodo pero de buen gusto, perfecto para una reunión relajada.
